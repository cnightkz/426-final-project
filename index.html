You can play a simple Black Jack here

Rules:
    1. Initially, you and dealer both will have two cards.
    2. You can choose to hit(get a new card) or stand(finish your turn and wait the dealer's result).
    3. If you choose to hit and your sum is Greater then 21, you loss.
    4. If you choose to stand, dealer will hit until dealer's sum is greater than 16.
    5. If dealer's sum is less than your sum, or dealer's sum is greater than 21, you win.
    6. You can change A's value by clicking the button. A's value can be 1 or 11.
